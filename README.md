### Welcome to Our Blood Bank Platform!

Our platform helps people find blood donors quickly and share donor information easily. You can:

Search donors by blood group and location.
Register yourself as a donor and keep your profile updated.
Add friends or family donors without them needing to register.
If a donor later registers, their profile is automatically linked to them.

This system makes it faster and easier to connect donors with those in need, all in one safe and simple platform.
